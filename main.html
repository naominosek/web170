The Game

You play as a member of a band of mercenaries. Throughout your adventure, you constantly decide which side to support, whether for monetary reasons or for ideological ones. Your choices throughout the game affect how the story plays out. Whether to try and defend the remaining humans from being murdered by the Boros by joining the Conclave, to save mankind and restore balance to the world by joining the Boros, or to somehow find out the secret of Rakdos.
